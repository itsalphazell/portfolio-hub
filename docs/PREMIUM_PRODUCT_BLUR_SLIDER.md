# Premium Product Blur Slider

Source inspiration: `Animmaster/Blur-Slider`

Use this preset when a campaign or product page needs one strong product spotlight with blur-led motion and clean buying context.

Use it for:

- product launch sections
- campaign landing pages
- showcase pages with 1 to 5 hero products

Do not use it for:

- dense catalog pages
- tables or utilitarian product comparison surfaces
- pages where accessibility would suffer from hidden navigation affordances

Files:

- `react/presets/ProductBlurSlider.tsx`
- `static/html/product-blur-slider.html`
- `static/css/product-blur-slider.css`
- `static/js/product-blur-slider.js`

Expected effect:

- product-led motion with clear price and CTA context
- cinematic blur transition without a heavy slider dependency
- reusable premium-web campaign pattern
