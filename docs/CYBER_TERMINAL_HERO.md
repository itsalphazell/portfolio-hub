# Cyber Terminal Hero

Source inspiration: `Karthigaiselvam-dev`

Use this preset when a technical portfolio, security brand, or infrastructure-facing landing page needs a stronger identity than a neutral editorial hero, but does not justify a full immersive scene.

Use it for:

- cybersecurity portfolios
- infrastructure or DevOps personal sites
- technical founder pages
- premium trust-led landing pages with one terminal-led visual system

Do not use it for:

- general lifestyle brands
- product pages that need one clear product spotlight
- sites where the terminal metaphor is decorative but not relevant

Files:

- `react/presets/CyberTerminalHero.tsx`
- `static/html/cyber-terminal-hero.html`
- `static/css/cyber-terminal-hero.css`
- `static/js/cyber-terminal-hero.js`

Expected effect:

- stronger technical identity without escalating to 3D
- terminal-led hero framing with controlled neon motion
- proof-first premium presentation for technical audiences
