# Perspective Tilt Headline

Source inspiration: `bosworthco/YWBLpR`

Use this pattern when the headline needs tactile depth but the page should still stay lightweight and conversion-safe.

Use it for:

- premium hero copy
- campaign intros
- editorial landing sections

Do not use it for:

- dense pricing tables
- utility pages
- pages already carrying a heavy immersive stage

Files:

- `react/components/PerspectiveTiltHeadline.tsx`
- `static/html/perspective-tilt-headline.html`
- `static/css/perspective-tilt-headline.css`
- `static/js/perspective-tilt-headline.js`

Expected effect:

- subtle perspective depth
- richer headline feel without scene overhead
- premium motion that stays readable under hover
