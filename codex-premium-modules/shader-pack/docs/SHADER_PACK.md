# Shader Pack

Use this pack for one atmospheric background or stage accent, not the whole site.

## Best fit
- premium-web marketing hero with stronger atmosphere
- premium-immersive hero or showcase backdrop
- static sites that need depth without jumping straight to full Three scenes

## Do not use when
- the page is already visually dense
- copy and CTA hierarchy are still unresolved
- product screens need predictable interaction and scanning

## Repo policy
- marketing React: allowed in `auto`
- marketing static: allowed in `auto`
- app-saas: docs only in `auto`, explicit opt-in per marketing surface

## QA gates
- headline still reads against the backdrop
- CTA contrast survives motion and glow
- the page still works with the shader removed
